Main price panel

Price candles, trade circles, regime zones, and optional resistance bands.

Candles show the selected bar interval. Circles mark individual or accumulated trades, with size proportional to trade size. The current price is kept vertically centered. Regime zones are a simple, reliable summary of recent flow: directional impulse, buy pressure, sell pressure, or chop. Orange lines show estimated support and resistance likelihood across the full chart width. Stronger evidence becomes more opaque, weak evidence stays very faint.
